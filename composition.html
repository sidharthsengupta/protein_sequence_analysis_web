Amino Acid Composition

This page will determine the percent composition of four classes of amino acids as well as individual counts. It will also determine estimated molecular weight and isoelectric point. The functionality is loosely based on the GMAW lite bioinformatics tool.

Methodology: The estimated molecular weight is the sum of all dehydrated amino acid weights. The estimated isoelectric point is the average of the individual isoelectric points.

Usage: Paste your sequence into the area below. If your sequence contains spaces or line breaks, please use the clean button to tidy your sequence before analyzing. Please allow up to five minutes for data to generate - especially for sequences longer than 500 residues.
